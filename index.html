Welcome to my modding guides, which cover mods and best practices for high quality and performant gaming! If you're just looking for a modlist or a tutorial, select a game on the left.

Please note:
Guides are usually written for Steam copies.

Why modding?

Modding is a great way to enhance your gaming experience. It can add new content, improve graphics, fix bugs, and more. However, modding can be complex and time-consuming, so it's important to follow a guide to ensure you're doing it correctly. If all goes well, you'll have a game that looks and plays better than ever before.

Do I need to pay?

You might think you need to pay for something like this, however, most mods are free, but some may ask for a small donation to the creator. This is usually optional, and you can still use the mod without paying. However, if you enjoy a mod, consider supporting the creator to help them continue making great content.

There's an exception:
Some mods are paid only or require a paid DLC to work. If any of that is the case, it will be mentioned in your guide. In other cases, it will be stated you need all or some DLC to follow the entire guide.
